## Create Your Account and Log In

Follow these steps to get your Ranked AI account up and running.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Your First Project" icon="badge-plus-icon" title-type="p">
    Navigate to <a href="https://app.ranked.ai/dashboard/projects/add" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https\://app.ranked.ai/dashboard/projects/add</a> to select your service and add your details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill Out Onboarding" icon="user" title-type="p">
    Provide your content and keyword preferences, connect your Google Search Console along with website access if you'd like us to handle implementation for you.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait For Our Plan" icon="timer-icon" title-type="p">
    We'll get back to you in a few working days with a plan for your site along with the work schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>
