## Overview

You can create a new project in Ranked AI in a few simple steps.

## Steps

1. Go to your Ranked AI dashboard.
2. Click the "New Project" button.
3. Enter your project name and select a template (if desired).
4. Click "Create".

Your project will appear on your dashboard. You can now add datasets, invite team members, or configure AI models as needed.
