## Overview

Follow these steps to launch your first AI project with Ranked AI.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up or log in">
    Navigate to https://ranked.ai and create your account or log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first project">
    Click "New Project" and enter your project details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add data">
    Upload a dataset or connect to your data source.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run your first analysis">
    Select an AI model and run an analysis to see immediate results.
  </Step>
</Steps>
