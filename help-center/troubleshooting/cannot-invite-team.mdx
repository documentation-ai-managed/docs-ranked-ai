---
title: Cannot Invite Team Members
description: Resolve issues with inviting users to your workspace.
---

## Symptoms

- Invite email not sent or not received
- Error: "Unable to send invitation"

## Causes

- Incorrect email address
- Invite limit reached
- Email ended in spam/junk folder

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check email spelling">
    Verify the email address you entered is correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check spam/junk folder">
    Ask the invitee to check their email spam or junk folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resend invitation">
    Click the "Resend Invite" option in your team settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>
